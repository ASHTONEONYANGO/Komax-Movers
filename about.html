About

Komax Movers is a locally owned and operated company based in Nairobi, Kenya. We have been helping clients with their moving and storage needs for over 5 years, and have a team of experienced professionals who are dedicated to providing top-quality service. Our services include trucking, packaging, logistics, warehousing, and storage, and we are committed to helping you with all aspects of your move. We use modern, well-maintained trucks and equipment, and offer secure, climate-controlled warehouses for your storage needs. Contact us today to learn more about how we can help you with your next move.
